Welcome to ENSTA Algiers

The National Higher School of Advanced Technologies (ENSTA) was established under Executive Decree No. 22-314 of September 12, 2022, published in the Official Journal No. 78. In the same issue, Executive Decree No. 22-398 of November 21, 2022 announced the dissolution of the National Higher School of Technology and the Higher School of Applied Sciences of Algiers, with the transfer of their assets, rights, obligations, staff, and resources to ENSTA. ENSTA is a public institution of a scientific, cultural, and professional nature, endowed with legal personality and financial autonomy. Its mission is to provide higher education, scientific research, and technological development in various fields of advanced technologies, including industrial engineering, transport engineering, automation, electronics, telecommunications, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and process engineering. Starting from the 2023-2024 academic year, in addition to its specialization in the Science and Technology field, ENSTA has introduced a new domain ‚Äî Mathematics and Computer Science (MI) ‚Äî which is highly in demand in both economic and practical sectors. ENSTA stands as a center of excellence in higher education, ensuring high-level training to serve the needs of the country's economic and industrial sectors.
